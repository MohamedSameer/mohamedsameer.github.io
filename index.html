    • Dr Mohamed is currently working as an Assistant Professor of computer engineering at Gachon University, South Korea and as AI & CV Senior Researcher at DeltaX Co., Ltd. , South Korea. He got his BSc from Al-Azhar University in 2004. He got his MSc and his PhD from Cairo University, in 2012 and 2020 respectively. From 2006 to 2014, he was working as a Teaching Assistant then an Assistant lecturer at Higher Institute of computer sciences, Egypt. From 2014 to 2020, he was working as a research Assistant at Electronics Research Institute (ERI), Egypt. Previously,he was a postdoctoral research fellow at Gachon University,South Korea, in 2022, where I worked with Prof. Young Im Cho. Dr Mohamed is on leave from Electronics Research Institute (ERI), Egypt. Dr Mohamed is a member of IEEE and has been a regular reviewer of international conferences and journals. He has published over 30 papers, developed and provided consultancy for several IT/IS projects. His research interest includes Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, Machine Learning, and data analytics.
